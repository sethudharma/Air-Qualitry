AIR POLLUTION AWARENESS

What is Air Pollution?

Air pollution refers to harmful substances in the air, posing health risks and environmental damage.

Sources of Air Pollution

Pollutants from vehicles, factories, and agriculture are major contributors to poor air quality.

Effects of Air Pollution

Long-term exposure to polluted air can cause respiratory issues, heart diseases, and environmental degradation.

How to Reduce Pollution

Limiting the use of fossil fuels, promoting renewable energy, and planting trees are key solutions to combat air pollution.

Health Impact of Air Pollution

Polluted air leads to respiratory problems, asthma, and in severe cases, can increase mortality rates.

Role of Technology in Reducing Pollution

Innovations like air quality sensors, electric vehicles, and air purifiers are helping to mitigate pollution effects.
